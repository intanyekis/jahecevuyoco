Mother Beverage (Poppi) Shark Tank Founder, Net Worth and Investment

What is Mother Beverage (Poppi) and Who Founded Mother Beverage (Poppi)?

Mother Beverage, also known as Poppi, is a carbonated beverage flavored with apple cider vinegar. It is renowned for its light, fruity, and bubbly taste. The founders of Mother Beverage (Poppi) are Stephen Ellsworth and Allison. Despite having no prior experience in the beverage industry, they embarked on their entrepreneurial journey and started the company in 2016.

Allison’s personal struggle with stomach issues became the inspiration behind creating a more appealing apple cider drink, which eventually led to the creation of Mother Beverage (Poppi). Understanding the importance of gut health, the drink contains prebiotics and is infused with apple cider vinegar, known for its benefits in digestion and promoting gut health.

Poppi offers a range of flavors including Orange, Strawberry Lemonade, Peach Tea, and Lime Ginger. These natural flavors contribute to the drink’s refreshing taste while providing additional benefits for the body.

The Founders’ Story

“Our dedication to making the health benefits of apple cider vinegar accessible and enjoyable has driven the success of Mother Beverage (Poppi).” – Stephen Ellsworth, Co-founder of Mother Beverage (Poppi)

In their pursuit of better health, Allison’s journey with stomach issues led her and Stephen to combine their passion for wellness and entrepreneurship. They sought to create a beverage that would not only taste great but also provide health benefits. Today, Mother Beverage (Poppi) has become a popular choice for those seeking a refreshing drink that supports gut health and overall well-being.

Mother Beverage (Poppi) Shark Tank Pitch

During their captivating pitch on Shark Tank, Stephen and Allison presented their innovative product to the panel of esteemed sharks. They showcased the unique apple cider vinegar-infused beverage and effectively highlighted its exceptional health benefits. Seeking a $400,000 investment for a 10% stake in the company, Stephen and Allison demonstrated their passion for their brand and its potential.

The Sharks were immediately impressed by the taste of the beverage and its remarkable sales performance, with the company generating $500,000 in sales. However, they expressed concerns regarding the need for comprehensive marketing efforts and the lack of proprietary elements that could differentiate Mother Beverage (Poppi) in the market.

“We love the refreshing taste and your enthusiasm, but we believe there is a need for strong marketing strategies to propel this product to new heights. We also see an opportunity for unique branding and proprietary aspects that will set you apart from competitors.”

Rohan Oza, a renowned guest shark known for his expertise in product marketing and brand building, recognized the potential of Mother Beverage (Poppi) and made a compelling offer. He presented Stephen and Allison with a lucrative proposition: $400,000 in exchange for a 25% stake in the company. Recognizing the immense opportunity for growth and the value that Rohan’s experience and network could bring, the co-founders decided to accept the offer.

This strategic partnership with Rohan Oza not only provided the necessary investment to fuel expansion but also opened doors to valuable connections within the industry. This collaboration enabled Mother Beverage (Poppi) to significantly increase its distribution reach and secure additional funding from CAVU, further accelerating the company’s growth trajectory.

Through their impactful presentation and compelling pitch, Stephen and Allison successfully secured the investment and guidance needed to propel Mother Beverage (Poppi) towards widespread success and establish it as a prominent player in the healthy beverage market.
